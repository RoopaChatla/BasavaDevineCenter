Activities


Monthly Services

Basava Divine Center conducts regular Monthly Services, typically on the third Sundays of each month. The Service includes:

  • Worship and Prayer
  • Discourses on the Path of Basavanna
  • Discussion on Vachanas
  • Meditation

The services are open to both adults and children. Active participation of children is encouraged.
