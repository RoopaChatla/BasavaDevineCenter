About BDC

Basava Divine Center is established in the USA to forge the royal spiritual path of Guru Basavanna and to spread his message globally. Currently, it operates from Austin, TX in the USA and Bangalore in India.

The vision of Basava Divine Center is to cultivate a global community of spiritual practitioners in the footsteps of Guru Basavanna and Sharanas by:

  • Popularizing the messages of Guru Basavanna and Sharanas.
  • Guiding aspirants in spiritual practices given by Guru Basavanna.
  • Facilitating the Indian American Lingayat community by offering religious services.

Poojya Allamaprabhu Swamiji

Poojya Allamaprabhu Swamiji is the driving force behind Basava Divine Center is. A scholar in Vachanas and Basava Philosophy, he is an accomplished practitioner and researcher of Basavayoga. He is dedicated to the cause of researching and propagating the percepts and practices of Guru Basavanna towards the betterment of individuals and society.

Poojya Swamiji is well versed in various systems of Indian philosophy and mystic traditions. An accomplished yogi, he is an expert in different forms of yoga. Known for his simple lifestyle and calm personality, he has embodied the percept of samate (mental equanimity), a key concept of Sharanas that ensues serenity into the life

Poojya Swamiji has authored books on Vachanas and Sharanas. He is also the editor of Basava Kirana, a monthly newspaper. He is a great orator and has extensively lectured across Karnataka spreading the eternal message of Vachanas. His elucidating lectures target all classes of the society and have won the acclaim of people from diverse background.

Rupaka

Along with Poojya Allamaprabhu Swamiji, Rupaka has been instrumental in establishing Basava Divine Center. A spiritual soul from her childhood, Rupaka has practiced various disciplines like Vedanta, Yoga, and Shakti Upasane prior to entering the path of Guru Basavanna. She is an Engineer by profession and is the founding CEO of a technology company. True to the traditions of Guru Basavanna, she is performing her Sadhana, Kayaka, and propagation of Basava teachings with dedication.

She has undertaken systematic study of Vachanas under the guidance of Poojya Swamiji. She has authored books and articles on Vachanas in both English and Kannada. She lectures and conducts classes in meditation, Basava Philosophy, and its application to life.
